About Me 

My name is Nguyen Vu Tien Dung or also known as Ben as a nickname I’ve used ever since I was born. I am turning 18 years old this year, and I’m from Vietnam. 

Throughout my life, I’ve been travelling and moving around to other countries once in every few years due to my mother’s occupation. I’ve lived in Singapore, and Myanmar long enough to call it my second home apart from Vietnam, and the Netherlands slowly joining that list. While traveling, living, and experiencing many cultures first hand, I’ve also been fascinated by all the differences between us as humans, no matter the place, everytime has something unique to themselves, to their culture. Since experiencing these cultures, from the strictly controlled Singaporean mentality to the peaceful and caring social aspects of Myanmar, I’ve taken interest in studying them.
 
I took upon UXD course in the Netherlands as I believe it is the first step towards learning about different aspects and cultural boundries. It is very interesting to study how people from different parts of the world think and to how we should design for them. These factors are what motivate me to strive to become a relatable, smart, and successful designer with unique simplelistic designs sutiable for many cultures.
